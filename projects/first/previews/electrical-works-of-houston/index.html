FAQ

Common Questions, Straight Answers

No fluff. If you don't see your question here, call us — we pick up every time.

How much does AC repair cost in Houston?
AC repair in Houston typically costs between $150 and $500 depending on the issue. Minor refrigerant recharges or capacitor replacements fall on the lower end, while compressor or coil repairs run higher. Arctic Air uses flat-rate pricing — you get a written quote before any work begins, so there are never any surprises.
How quickly can you respond to an emergency HVAC call?
Our average emergency response time is under 2 hours anywhere in the Houston metro area. We operate 24/7, 365 days a year — including weekends and holidays — because we know your comfort can't wait. Same-day service is available for the vast majority of repairs.
Do you offer a warranty on HVAC repairs?
Yes. All parts we install come with a 10-year manufacturer warranty, and all labor is covered by our 30-day guarantee. If the same issue returns within 30 days of your repair, we come back and fix it at no charge — no questions asked. That's our promise.
What areas do you serve around Houston?
We serve the entire Houston metro area including Aurora, Lakewood, Englewood, Centennial, Arvada, Westminster, Thornton, Littleton, Parker, Castle Rock, and Highlands Ranch. If you're within 30 miles of downtown Houston, we've got you covered with the same fast response time.
Is it worth repairing my old AC unit or should I replace it?
As a general rule, if your system is over 10 years old and the repair cost exceeds 50% of a new unit's price, replacement is often the smarter investment — especially with today's energy-efficient models. Our technicians will give you an honest, side-by-side cost comparison. We show you the math and let you decide. No pressure, ever.
