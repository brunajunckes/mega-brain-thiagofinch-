FAQ

Answers to Common Questions

Straight answers — no runaround, no vague contractor-speak.

How much does commercial construction cost per square foot in Denver?
Commercial construction in Denver typically ranges from $150 to $350+ per square foot depending on the building type, finish level, and site conditions. Basic office shell space runs $150–$200/sq ft. Medical or lab space with specialized infrastructure can reach $300–$400/sq ft. We provide itemized estimates after a free on-site assessment so you know exactly what you're paying for — before you commit to anything.
How long does a commercial building project typically take?
Project timelines vary significantly by scope. A tenant improvement or interior renovation might take 6–12 weeks. A ground-up commercial building of 10,000–30,000 sq ft typically takes 8–18 months from permit approval to certificate of occupancy. We provide a detailed milestone schedule during the estimate process so you have exact dates — not ranges — to plan around.
Do you handle permits and inspections?
Yes, completely. Our team manages all permit applications, plan submissions, and inspection scheduling with local authorities across the Denver Metro. This is included in every project at no additional fee. We've established working relationships with all 12 jurisdictions we serve, which typically means faster approval timelines than projects pulled by owners directly.
What is your payment schedule for construction projects?
Our standard payment schedule is structured around project milestones, not arbitrary calendar dates. Typical structure: 10% deposit at contract execution, 25% at foundation/framing completion, 25% at rough mechanical/electrical/plumbing, 25% at drywall and exterior close, and 15% at final punch list completion. We never require more than 10% upfront, and we never draw ahead of work completed.
Are you licensed and insured in Colorado?
Yes. Princeton Construction holds Colorado General Contractor License #GC-2024-7891, issued by the Colorado Division of Professions and Occupations. We carry $5M commercial general liability insurance, workers' compensation covering all employees and subcontractors, and a $1M performance bond. A full certificate packet is provided before any work begins on your project.
