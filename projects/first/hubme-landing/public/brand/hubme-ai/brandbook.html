05 // Do & Don't
Do
Logo on dark background — full contrast, brand colors fully legible.
Don't
Logo on busy or colorful backgrounds — mark is lost and brand integrity collapses.
Do
One accent: Cyan only
#22D3EE as the singular brand accent. One color, full identity.
Don't
Multiple accent colors mixed
Mixing accents dilutes the brand. Cyan is singular and ownable.
Do
Correct proportions maintained — mark and wordmark at original aspect ratio.
Don't
Never stretch or squash the logo. Scale uniformly — always preserve the original aspect ratio.
